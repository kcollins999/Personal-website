About Me

Reading

I really enjoy a good book. It could be informational or fiction. I like suspense and thiller books. I feel they create a good storyline. I also enjoy books with comedy and drama. I love informative books on nutrition and health. Learning about different herbs for certain illness and sickness or just overall good health.

Traveling

If I could travel the world for a living I would. I find beauty in exploring the world around me that I don't get to see everyday. Learning and embracing different cultures always puts a smile on my face. Tropical resorts are my favorite to visit.

Fashion

I love fashion and styling. I like the idea of putting outfits together and wearing them with confidence. With fashion you really can't go wrong. It's another form of art. Meaning you have to make it your own. It constantly changes and is customizable. The way someone styles themselves reveals a lot about them.
